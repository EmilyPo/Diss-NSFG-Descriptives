Chapter 9 Alter Race Issues

9.1 Problem

alter race was not asked in a variety of situations

We HAVE alter race for all active partnerships. For MALE EGOS, we HAVE race for all previous cohabs and marriages. We also have a few one-time partner races for MALES if the one-time partner was their most recent partner.

We DO NOT HAVE alter race for any inactive partnerships or one-time partnerships for females. We DO NOT HAVE alter race for inactive “others” for males. We DO NOT HAVE alter race for male one-time partners that were not their most recent partner.
